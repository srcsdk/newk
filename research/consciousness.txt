cia research into consciousness and remote viewing

project stargate (1978-1995)
- dia/cia funded research at stanford research institute (sri)
- remote viewing: ability to perceive distant locations
- key researchers: hal puthoff, russell targ, ingo swann
- declassified 1995 under freedom of information act

source documents:
- cia-rdp96-00789r003300210001-2 (stargate collection)
- cia.gov/readingroom/collection/stargate

project mkultra (1953-1973)
- cia program for behavioral modification research
- 149 sub-projects across universities and institutions
- declassified through foia requests and church committee (1975)
- 20,000 documents released 1977

source documents:
- cia.gov/readingroom/collection/mkultra
- church committee report, book 1, chapter 17

project artichoke (1951-1953)
- predecessor to mkultra
- interrogation techniques and behavioral control
- renamed from project bluebird

monroe institute connection:
- robert monroe collaborated with cia on gateway process
- hemi-sync technology evaluated for intelligence applications
- focus 15 (no-time state) investigated for remote viewing potential
