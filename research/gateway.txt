cia gateway process - analysis and assessment of gateway process
declassified 2003, approved for release 2003/09/10

source: cia.gov/readingroom/docs/CIA-RDP96-00788R001700210016-5.pdf

wayne m. mcdonnell, 1983
commander, us army intelligence and security command

hemi-sync (hemispheric synchronization)
- audio technique developed by robert monroe
- alters consciousness via binaural beat frequencies
- left and right ear receive slightly different frequencies
- brain produces a third frequency (the difference)
- entrains brainwave patterns to specific states

key concepts:
- consciousness as a vibratory system
- energy and matter as different forms of the same thing (planck, de broglie)
- holographic universe model (pribram, bohm)
- the absolute: universal consciousness field
- focus levels: 10 (mind awake/body asleep), 12 (expanded awareness), 15 (no time), 21 (other systems)

the holographic model:
- the brain is a holographic processor (pribram)
- the universe itself is holographic (bohm)
- each part contains information about the whole
- consciousness interacts with this holographic reality
- perception is a lens focusing on specific frequency bands

energy:
- consciousness generates energy in a toroidal pattern
- this energy interacts with the universal hologram
- altered states shift which frequencies consciousness processes
- the gateway process systematically trains this shift

implications per the report:
- time is not linear from the perspective of the absolute
- matter is frozen light (energy at rest)
- the universe is conscious and consciousness is fundamental
- human consciousness can access information non-locally
